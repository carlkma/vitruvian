VITRUVIAN

A Visualization Interface for Medical Data and Models

We introduce VITRUVIAN, a software interface for visualizations of medical data and models. By improving the efficiency of accessing and interpreting data, VITRUVIAN streamlines the clinical workflow with easier disease diagnosis and treatment.

The interface is centered around a digital avatar which presents a patient’s information anatomically. Clinician users of VITRUVIAN may interact with the digital avatar by clicking on an organ system to view the relevant vitals or lab test results.

Additionally, clinicians may feed these patient metrics into a library of machine learning models, or ask questions in natural language to retrieve intelligent summaries and insights of the patient’s condition.

Preliminary feedback of VITRUVIAN indicates that the ability for clinicians to visualize complex medical data and models is novel and beneficial.
